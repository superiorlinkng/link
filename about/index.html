About Us

Indigenous downstream trading & haulage — built for compliance, transparency & execution.

Superior Link Nigeria Ltd is a structured downstream trading and logistics company supplying PMS, AGO, Jet A1 and CNG with refinery-linked sourcing, disciplined documentation, and QHSE-led delivery operations.

Who We Are

We operate as a fully registered indigenous energy company focused on refined petroleum products and controlled logistics. Our model integrates trading, haulage, compliance, and documentation under one accountable framework.

We serve marketers, industrial users, aviation operators, distributors, and strategic partners across Lagos/Ogun, Abuja, and the South East.

Our Operating Standards

  • Refinery-linked sourcing & structured deal sheets
  • QHSE-led haulage & journey management
  • Audit-ready documentation (waybill, POD, job file)
  • Transparent pricing with validity windows
  • Compliance-first buyer onboarding

Our Mission

To deliver refined petroleum products with reliability, transparency, and disciplined execution — backed by strong compliance and operational governance.

Our Vision

To become a leading indigenous downstream operator known for trust, documentation discipline, and safe delivery operations across Nigeria and West Africa.

Leadership

How It Works

  1. We confirm your consumption pattern, tank capacity & delivery schedule.
  2. We issue a spot quote or propose a standing supply structure.
  3. Truck assignment, journey plan, loading & delivery to your site.
  4. POD, job file completion & periodic summaries for contract customers.

Documentation on Every Delivery

  • Supply proposal (for standing contracts)
  • Loading ticket / depot documents
  • Waybill & POD (signed delivery note)
  • Periodic supply summary (for contract customers)

Quality & Compliance

  • Strict discharge safety rules at customer sites
  • Quality assurance & documentation retention
  • Claims process backed by records
  • QHSE-led trucking & subcontractor controls

Pricing & Validity

Due to market volatility, pricing is issued as a validity-window quote (e.g., same-day). For repeat buyers, we can propose a weekly reset or index-based structure.
